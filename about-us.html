About Us

Sigma Phi Zeta, as a new comer brand, try to revive the human's passion with fashion and we design it to become your own experience. Through the making of our style of clothing and accesories, we bring the design that fits into the present lifestyle. It doesn’t matter who you are or where you live, Sigma Phi Zeta makes clothes that transcend all categories and social groups. Our clothes are made for all, going beyond age, gender, occupation, ethnicity, and all other ways that define people. Our clothes are simple and essential yet universal, so people can freely combine them in their own unique style.

Corporate History

Sigma Phi Zeta is founded in year 2009, with its operations and design team based in Kuala Lumpur. Currently it has more than 12 retail stores located in some of the biggest cities in Asia, and is rapidly expanding. Sigma Phi Zeta Fashion House is a subsidiary company of Premier Products Group. Premier Products Group is a multinational agency spanning over South East Asia, with markets ranging from Malaysia, Singapore to Taiwan, and they produce, distribute and retail various products from daily supplies, to gadgets and fashion.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How can I pay for my order when shopping on sigmaphizeta.com?

A: We accept payments online through major credit/debit cards, such as Visa, MasterCard, American Express, and more. Please call our customer care line for more information on the cards that we accept. We will only charge your card once we accepted your order and deliver the invoice.

Q: After I made payment, can I make changes to my order?

A: No. As we start to process your order as soon as we receive it, hence we are unable to cancel or change your order. However, if you changed your mind after placing your order, you may return any items that comply with our Return Policy within 14 days by shipping it back to us, and we will help you to process a refund.

Q: Can I order online and have in store pick ups instead?

A: No. In store pick-ups are not available for online orders.

Q: What is your return policy?

A: Our return policy states that for online purchases, you would be able to return it through mail shipping within 14 days, so long as the goods are still in good resaleable condition and with its original tags. However, you could also bringing the original receipt, and drop by any of our official retail stores and request for a return.

Q: How can I figure out my size when shopping online?

A: We have a Sizing Chart that may be of assistance and provide reference for your online shopping needs.
