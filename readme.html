Description

This website is made by Loh Hao Bin (25461257) and Christophorus Souw (25306545) for FIT1012 Web Authoring Assignment 2, which assigned us to produce a retail website for a fictional client named Premier Products. In our case, Premier Products is the parent company of this fashion outlet named Sigma Phi Zeta, which we made this website for. The design of this website is tailored to fit their brand image of being a leading high street fashion house, being easy to use and navigate, as well as being clean and minimal throughout to shift the focus of the user on the content.

The target audience for the new website is for men and women of the age group 16-35, who are interested in knowing more about fashion designs of Sigma Phi Zeta and people who wish to purchase fashion online.

Testing

This website is best viewed on a minimum resolution of 1024x768, on a modern web browser such as Google Chrome 30+, Firefox 17+, Safari 6+, Opera 15+ and IE9/10+. Certain elements may not be optimised for older browser versions such as IE7/8. This site has also been tested on Safari on iOS 7.0+, and Android 4.0+ browser.
